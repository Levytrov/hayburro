Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

We may also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version

  • Pages visited

  • Date and time of visits

  • Website usage and interaction data

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Provide newsletters, updates, and communications

  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Analyze website performance and engagement

  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and interests

Email Communications

If you provide your email address, we may send you:

  • Newsletters

  • Invitations
  • Marketing communications
  • Product updates
  • Special offers and promotions

  • Measure website performance
  • Analyze visitor behavior

  • Support marketing and advertising efforts

  • Cloud storage

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Consent

  • Proteger nuestros derechos e intereses

Comunicaciones por Correo Electrónico
